About Me

I am an Associate Professor of Economics at Imperial College Business School. My current research uses unstructured data to build new measures of economic activity and behavior across a variety of applications, most often related to organizational economics and monetary policy. I maintain a Github page where I share code and lecture slides related to methodologies for the analysis of unstructured and high-dimensional data. I co-organize the monthly AMLEDS webinar that explores economic applications of machine learning. My research is supported by an ERC Conslidator Grant.
